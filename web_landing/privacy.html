Privacy Policy

Hidden AI Tech · Last updated June 2026

This document explains what Hidden AI Tech (“the app”) collects, why, and what we don’t do. We've kept it short on purpose.

What we collect

  • Account info: when you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, profile photo URL, and Google account ID. Stored in Firebase Authentication.
  • Profile: your selected profession, interest topics, and country. Stored at users/{your-id} in Firestore. Only you can read or write your own profile.
  • Comments you post: text, your display name, your photo URL, and a timestamp. Visible to everyone using the app.
  • Local-only data: pinned topics, vault saves, and which cards you've already seen. Stored only on your device via SharedPreferences. We can’t see this.
  • Coarse location (only if you tap the Nearby chip and grant permission): the latitude/longitude of your device, reverse-geocoded to a city name. Cached on your device for 24 hours. We don’t send raw coordinates anywhere.

What we don’t collect

  • No analytics SDKs, no crash reporting, no marketing pixels.
  • No fine GPS, no background location, no movement history.
  • No contacts, no microphone, no camera, no SMS.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

Third-party services we call

  • Firebase (Google): authentication, profile storage, comment storage. Subject to Firebase Privacy.
  • Pollinations AI: the prompt you submit (chat questions, "Explain Simply" presets) is sent anonymously to text.pollinations.ai to generate explanations. Your account info is never sent.
  • News sources: arXiv, GitHub, Hacker News, Reddit, Dev.to, and ~22 RSS feeds. Each request is anonymous, sent only when you load the relevant tab.
  • Image scraping: when a card needs a thumbnail we fetch the article page once to read its og:image meta tag. The publisher's server sees an anonymous request.
  • Google News RSS: when you tap "Nearby", we send your city name (not raw coordinates) to news.google.com to get a list of headlines.

Your controls

  • Sign out any time from Settings. Your local data stays on the device until you reinstall.
  • Edit profile from Settings to change profession, topics, or country.
  • Clear seen history and empty intel vault are one-tap actions in Settings.
  • Delete your account + data: email us (see contact below) and we'll remove your Firestore user document and all comments you've posted.
  • Revoke location permission any time from your phone's Settings → Apps → Hidden AI → Permissions.

Data retention

  • Profile and comments persist until you delete them or your account.
  • The Nearby city cache is automatically discarded after 24 hours.
  • Firebase Authentication records are kept until you request deletion.

Children's privacy

Hidden AI is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we'll delete it.

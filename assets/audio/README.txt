Glint "Live Listen" news-channel background music.

Drop ONE royalty-free instrumental loop here named exactly:

    news_bed.mp3

It plays quietly (10% volume) under the AI anchor during a Live Listen
broadcast — like a TV news bed. Live Listen works fine WITHOUT it (the code
silently skips the bed if the file is missing).

Where to get a free, license-safe loop (check each license for app use):
  • Pixabay Music     https://pixabay.com/music/search/news/      (CC0-ish, no attribution)
  • Uppbeat           https://uppbeat.io/browse/genre/corporate   (free tier, get a clear-code)
  • Free Music Archive https://freemusicarchive.org/               (filter by CC license)

Tips:
  • Keep it instrumental and low-energy so it doesn't fight the voice.
  • A 30–90s seamless loop is plenty (it loops automatically).
  • Re-run `flutter pub get` after adding the file, then rebuild.
